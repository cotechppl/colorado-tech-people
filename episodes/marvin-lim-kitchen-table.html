Building a Marketplace Means Building Two Businesses at Once

Marvin Lim of The Kitchen Table joins Colorado Tech People to discuss what it really takes to build a marketplace — from finding the right ICP to balancing supply, demand, trust, hospitality, and experience.

About this episode

Building a marketplace sounds exciting — until you realize you’re building two businesses at once. In this conversation, Marvin Lim shares lessons from building The Kitchen Table, a platform connecting people with private chef dining experiences.

The conversation explores marketplace dynamics, customer discovery, hospitality, affordability, luxury experiences, supply and demand, and what founders learn when their first ICP assumptions do not fully hold.
